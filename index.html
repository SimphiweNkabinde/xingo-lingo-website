Our Story

I’m Vuyisa Sigonyela — linguist, creative entrepreneur, and a passionate believer in the power of language to unite and transform. South Africa’s linguistic landscape is rich, diverse, and full of stories waiting to be heard. But too many learners struggle to read for meaning- it is a national crisis! In South Africa 80% of grade 4 learners cannot read for meaning, immigrants face barriers in connecting with local communities, and tourists miss out on the true depth of our culture.

That’s why my team and I created this app — not just as a language learning tool, but as a solution to a national crisis in our country and a bridge between people. Our app empowers learners at the grassroots level to conquer literacy challenges, helping young South Africans unlock their potential through the magic of language. It supports newcomers, especially the growing American expat community, to engage confidently with their new home. And it invites tourists to experience South Africa beyond the surface — to truly feel our culture by speaking the lingo.

More than words, this app is a gateway to understanding, connection, and inclusion. My vision as I grow the brand is to develop it to serve not just South Africa but all of the continent of Africa’s market. This is a celebration of who we are and where we come from, produced with care by someone who knows language is not just communication — it’s identity, heritage, and community.
